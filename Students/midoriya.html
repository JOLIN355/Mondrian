Biography
Izuku Midoriya, also known as Deku, is a determined and hardworking student at U.A. High School. He possesses the quirk One For All, which grants him
incredible strength and agility. Despite being born without a quirk, he never gave up on his dream of becoming a hero and has inspired many with his
perseverance and bravery.
