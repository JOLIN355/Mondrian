Biography
Fumikage Tokoyami is known for his calm demeanor and his quirk, Dark Shadow, which allows him to summon
a shadowy creature that can manipulate darkness. He often uses this ability for stealth and surprise attacks.
Tokoyami is a loyal friend and values his connections with others.
