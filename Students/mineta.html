Biography
Minoru Mineta is known for his quirky personality and his unique quirk, Pop Off, which allows him to create sticky balls from his head. Despite his perverted nature,
he is a loyal friend and a determined student at U.A. High School. He often provides comic relief in the series with his antics and humorous attempts to flirt with girls.
